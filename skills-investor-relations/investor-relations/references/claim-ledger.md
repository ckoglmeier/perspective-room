## High-Risk Claim Types

- Round amount, valuation, instrument, and close timing.
- Revenue, ARR/MRR, pipeline, retention, usage, and customer counts.
- Customer names, contracts, pilots, and distribution relationships.
- Benchmark or AI capability claims.
- Market size and category leadership claims.
- Regulatory, security, privacy, and compliance statements.
- Team backgrounds and prior outcomes.
